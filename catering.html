Catering

Let us do the worrying for you!

Perfect for an office lunch or informal gathering! Whether it’s a meeting break, tailgate party or wedding reception, the Stefano’s caters to you!

On our Catering Menu you will find some selections from our regular restaurant menu, available in larger portions. The same quality food you get in the restaurant, but portioned to feed your crowd.

This menu is extremely popular with our corporate clients that order office lunches and dinners as well as our residential clients that order for a birthday party or any special occasion. Prepared cuisine is available for pick-up at Winter Springs, accompanied by safe reheating instructions. Stefano’s Trattoria is always certain to please the crowd and make the host look great!
