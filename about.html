MEANING OF TRATTORIA:
trat·to·ri·a
/trätəˈrēə/ noun: trattoria; plural noun: trattorias
An Italian restaurant serving homestyle comfort food. Here at Stefano’s Trattoria we offer Time, Quality, and Passion, the best ingredients for an exquisite meal. Quality and Passion we do have. Time you have to give us. We are very delighted to have you as our guests. We hope our love and dedication to this restaurant will add to the pleasure of your meal.

Pizzas, Calzones, Strombolis and more.....

We have some of the best bakes in the world. From basic pizza margarita to authentic old school family favorites such as the Tortellini Di Stefano and baked ziti. What will You have us bake you today!?

World Renowned Appetizers!

Stefano's has been making the best apps for years now. Some tried and true like brushetta and fried calamari, to new innovations like stuffed mushrooms with balsamic glaze mmmm!

The staff at Stefano's has always been family!
